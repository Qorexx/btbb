If your team is building under the Convex theme, please go through the following resources before you begin development. These resources are designed to help you understand the platform, speed up development, and solve issues quickly during the hackathon.
Step 1: Get Started with Convex
Open the link below and click "Start Building." Follow the complete tutorial to learn:
* How to set up a Convex project
* Connecting your frontend with the backend
* Creating and managing your database
* Writing queries, mutations, and actions
* Deploying your application
* Understanding the overall Convex development workflow
Completing this tutorial will give you a solid foundation and help you avoid common setup mistakes.
